About this website

 
Studying and staying focused is a difficult experience for many students. This becomes even harder when you don't have access to a calming environment like the library to help you focus.

 
My study space aims to provide a calming space to for studying that users can access anywhere. Study space features calming sound effects and to-do list to help you stay focused. Chrome is recommended for best experiences.
